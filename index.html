How to Book Your First Slot

1

Go to Live Parking

Navigate to the "Live Parking" section or click "Find Parking" from the home page.

2

Select Floor

Choose your preferred floor (Ground, First, or Second) using the floor tabs.

3

Choose a Slot

Click on an available (green) parking slot. Selected slot will turn blue.

4

Enter Details

Fill in your vehicle details, date, time, and duration in the booking form.

5

Make Payment

Choose payment method and complete the secure payment process.

6

Confirmation

Receive booking confirmation via email and in your dashboard.

Pro Tips:

  • Book in advance for guaranteed availability
  • Use the mobile app for faster booking on the go
  • Save your vehicle details for future bookings
  • Check for EV charging slots if you have an electric vehicle
